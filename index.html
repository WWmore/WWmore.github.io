Official Web

Xi'an, China

I am an associate professor in the School of Mathematics and Stasitics of Xi’an Jiaotong University in China. Previously I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), working together with Prof. Helmut Pottmann and Prof. Shehab Ahmed . I got my PhD in 2020 in Computational Geometry group (PI: Prof. Chungang Zhu) from Dalian University of Technology (DUT). During my PhD study, I visited Prof. Helmut Pottmann’s Applied Geometry group in TU Wien for 2 years. My research interests include Computer Graphics, Computational Design and Fabrication, CAD/CAM, Architectural Geometry, Geometry Processing, Computational Geometry, Image Processing, 3D Reconstruction, CV, etc.

Welcome to contact me if you are interested in my research!
